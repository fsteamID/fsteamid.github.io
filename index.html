Are you a developer exploring an awesome name for your personal website and projects? You came to the right place!

gitdev.se.ke is a free identity for developers on the web. This service offers a handy subdomain service for you to have a subdomain like foo.gitdev.se.ke or bar.gitdev.se.ke for use on your static sites via CNAME DNS record. Fork, add subdomain and send a Pull Request. We will make it work!
